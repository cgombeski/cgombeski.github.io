Virtual Trivia is one of the only good things to come out of the Covid Pandemic

For us, virtual trivia started as a refuge from the news of the day back in winter 2020, when we put on a weekly virtual trivia game to keep us connected with our friends and try out different facial hair styles. It didn't have the same buzzing in the air as when two dozen trivia teams pack into a pub, but those virtual trivia nights had their joys too, which makes them perfect for corporate holiday parties, team-building activities, nonprofit fundraisers, or just the odd night of fun among friends scattered far and wide. Shoot us a message with details about the virtual trivia night you have in mind, and we promise to bring the fun - and to leave our ratty 2020 facial hair by the wayside.
