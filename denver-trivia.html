Hosting Denver Pub Trivia Nights Since 2018

What makes a good pub trivia night? Time, energy, and good beer. We aren't brewers, so we leave the third up to you, but we have those first items on lock. Having hosted more than our fair share of pub trivia nights, we're always chasing that quintessential pub trivia question, the one that has you noodling because you the answer's on the tip of your tongue, and that will leave you with a satisfied air when you nail it. For just a second, you're the smartest person on earth - or at least in the room - and that's enough. That's why every single question we write is tailor-made for your venue and audience. Because a good trivia night should work up a thirst, we believe in getting people up out of their seats, with games aplenty interspersed throughout the night. These take the form of limbo contests, talent shows, spelling bees, and lightning-fast lightning rounds that your bar patrons won't want to miss!
